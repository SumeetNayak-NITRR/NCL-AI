# Player Photo Upload Guide

## Image Requirements

For the best FIFA-style player cards, please prepare your photos as follows:

### 1. Remove Background First

**Recommended Tools:**
- **remove.bg** - https://remove.bg (best quality)
- **PhotoRoom** - https://photoroom.com
- **Photoshop** - Use Quick Selection + Delete
- **GIMP** (free) - Select by Color + Delete

### 2. Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG (with transparent background)
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1000x1000px (higher is better)
- **Subject**: Clear shot of player, ideally full body or upper body
- **Quality**: High resolution for best card appearance

### 3. Upload Process

1. Go to registration form
2. Click "Choose Photo" in Player Card Photo section
3. Upload your **pre-processed PNG** file
4. Crop as needed in the image editor
5. Submit registration

## Step-by-Step: Using Remove.bg

1. **Visit** https://remove.bg
2. **Upload** your player photo
3. **Download HD** version (free accounts have lower resolution)
   - For HD quality: Consider paid option ($0.20/image)
   - Or use other tools for better free quality
4. **Upload** the downloaded PNG to registration form

## Tips for Best Results

✅ **Do:**
- Use high-resolution source photos
- Ensure good lighting in original photo
- Stand against plain/contrasting background
- Upload PNG format after removing background

❌ **Don't:**
- Upload JPG with background (won't work for FIFA cards)
- Use low-resolution photos
- Upload photos with complex backgrounds

## Need Help?

If you're having trouble removing backgrounds, contact the admin who can assist or process images for you.

---

**Note:** The automatic background removal feature is disabled to preserve image quality. You have full control over the final image quality by preparing files yourself.
